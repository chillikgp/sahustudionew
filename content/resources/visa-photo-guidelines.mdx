# Visa Photo Guidelines

Visa photo requirements depend on the destination country, embassy, and application format. Before visiting the studio, keep the required size, background colour, file dimensions, and upload limit ready if your application has specific instructions.

At Sahu Studio, a [passport photo studio in Delhi](/passport-photo-delhi), we can help prepare clean visa and document photographs for print or digital submission when the exact requirement is shared in advance.
