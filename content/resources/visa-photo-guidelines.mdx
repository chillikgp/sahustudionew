# Visa Photo Guidelines

![Visa photo checklist for studio preparation](/images/services/passport-photo-delhi/visa-checklist.jpg?v=20260517)

Visa photo requirements depend on the destination country, embassy, and application format. There is no single universal visa photo size, so the safest approach is to share the country or embassy instruction before the photo is prepared.

## What to bring

Keep the required size, background colour, file dimensions, and upload limit ready if your application has specific instructions. Some portals ask for JPEG files, maximum file size, or a particular pixel dimension. Others need printed photographs for physical submission.

## Studio preparation

At Sahu Studio, a [passport photo studio in Delhi](/passport-photo-delhi), we can prepare clean visa and document photographs for print or digital submission when the exact requirement is shared in advance.
