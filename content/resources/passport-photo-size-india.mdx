# Passport Photo Size in India

![Passport photo size diagram for India](/images/services/passport-photo-delhi/size-diagram.jpg?v=20260517)

For Indian passport-style photos, many applicants use a practical 35 x 45 mm / 3.5 x 4.5 cm presentation. Exact requirements can vary by application flow, form, or online portal, so it is always wise to check the latest instruction before final submission.

## Background and framing

A good passport photograph should be a recent color image with a plain white background. The face should be photographed in full front view, the head should be centered, eyes should be open, and the expression should stay natural. Avoid distracting shadows, harsh retouching, tilted framing, or busy clothing near the face.

## Print quality

Printed passport photos should look clear, clean, and continuous-tone. That means the print should not appear pixelated, banded, overly sharpened, or washed out. A studio print is useful when you need consistent size, clean background, and reliable output on a photo sheet.
